About the hat
Make Denali The Great One Again with this foam trucker hat! Despite widespread opposition from Alaskans and even Alaska's Republican congressional delegation, Trump ignorantly signed an executive order to change the mountain's official name to Mount McKinley. Show your support for the mountain's original and true name with this hat!

  • Thicker and heavier fabric, laminated with high-density, non-toxic foam

  • Seamless foam front panel with lining
  • Matching fabric undervisor
  • Matching color braid and sweatband
